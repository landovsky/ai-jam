## **Epic 1: Public Presence & Discovery**

**Goal:** To communicate the value of the community and maintain a persistent, searchable knowledge base of AI "recipes."

### **1.1 User Story: Landing Page Discovery**

* **Description:** As an anonymous user, I want to view a landing page that explains the mission, monthly cadence, and format of AI Jam.
* **Motivation:** New visitors need to quickly understand if this meetup helps their personal or professional AI journey.
* **Acceptance Criteria:**
* [ ] The page displays a mission statement and a "How it Works" section.
* [ ] Primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons for "Join Community" and "Explore Recipes."
* [ ] The page is accessible without any authentication.



### **1.2 User Story: The Living Recipe Book**

* **Description:** As any user, I want to browse past topics formatted as "Recipes" to see historical value.
* **Motivation:** AI tools evolve rapidly; keeping these topics editable ensures the knowledge base remains current.
* **Acceptance Criteria:**
* [ ] Each Recipe displays a "Handle," "Description," and "Outcomes."
* [ ] Recipes remain editable by the Topic Manager or Admin indefinitely.
* [ ] Users can search or filter past recipes by keywords or tags.
